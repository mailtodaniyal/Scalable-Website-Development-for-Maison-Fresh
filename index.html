Our Story

Born from a passion for excellence and a love for the finest produce, Maison Fresh brings European market tradition to the heart of Toronto.

A New Chapter in Fresh Excellence

Nestled in the historic St Lawrence Market, Maison Fresh represents a harmonious blend of Old World charm and contemporary sophistication. Our journey begins with a simple yet profound commitment: to source and present only the most exceptional fresh produce and artisanal goods.

Inspired by the grand food halls of Europe and the vibrant market traditions of France, we've created a destination where quality is never compromised, and every visit becomes an experience in culinary discovery.

Our carefully curated selection spans from farm-fresh local produce to rare international delicacies, each item chosen for its superior quality, flavor, and provenance. We partner directly with trusted farmers, artisans, and specialty purveyors who share our unwavering dedication to excellence.
